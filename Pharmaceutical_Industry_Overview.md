## Industry Overview
The pharmaceutical industry operates in a highly regulated and competitive environment, with revenues driven by product innovation, market access, and effective sales execution. Companies manage large product portfolios across diverse therapeutic areas, regions, and customer segments.

## Sales Landscape
Pharmaceutical sales differ from traditional retail sales due to complex distribution channels involving hospitals, distributors, pharmacies, and institutional buyers. Sales performance is influenced by regional demand variability, physician adoption patterns, pricing controls, and sales force coverage.

## Importance of Analytics
Given the scale and complexity of operations, pharmaceutical organizations increasingly rely on data analytics to monitor performance, optimize sales strategies, and improve decision-making. Analytics supports better visibility into product performance, regional trends, and sales efficiency, enabling leadership to respond proactively to market changes.
